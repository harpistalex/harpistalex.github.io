An app to help my students practise their scales and memorise key signatures. Students can select their grade, choose which scales they wish to practice, and cycle through at random. There are over 2000 different scales and arpeggios as part of the ABRSM syllabus which I manage with a realm database. I’m currently working on an expansion to incorporate some machine learning, experimenting with CoreML and Tensorflow to record the user’s input and provide feedback.

TECHNOLOGY USED:

Swift, Xcode, Realm
