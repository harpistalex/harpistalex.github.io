This is a game I created for a friend’s 30th birthday party, where participants were able to play a scavenger-hunt style quiz game for the duration of the party, with their results posted to firebase. I was able to check users’ progress from an admin account, and announce the scores at the end of the day. I made this app first for iOS, before realising the party would be made up mostly of Cambridge mathematics graduates with little interest in Apple products and therefore brushed up on my Kotlin skills to have the app available natively for Android.

TECHNOLOGY USED:

Swift, Xcode, Kotlin, Android Studio, Firebase
