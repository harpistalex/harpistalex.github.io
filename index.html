About

I have been surrounded by computers and technology since the late 80’s, using MS-DOS to play computer games. My father first taught me to program in C using Visual C when I was 11 years old. My interest in computing continued throughout school, playing classic 90’s online puzzles and riddles, and creating websites with primitive versions of HTML and CSS.

For the past 13 years I have run a successful business as a professional harpist and teacher, performing all over the country at venues such as Glyndebourne Opera House and the Houses of Parliament, for the Royal Ascot, celebrities, royalty, and live on mainstream radio and television.

I am looking for a change in career as I have discovered over recent years that I find problem solving and developing more rewarding than performing in the service sector. I have spent the past 3 years honing my craft in mobile development, focusing on Swift and Kotlin.
